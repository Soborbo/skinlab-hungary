The Hydro-Facial (AquaPeeling) treatment is one of today's most popular, pain-free skin renewal procedures. The treatment cleanses, hydrates and revitalizes the skin all at once, which is why it can be offered to a wide range of clients all year round.

The aim of our training is for you not only to master operating the device, but to understand how the skin works, the role of active ingredients and the protocols with which you can achieve striking results.

## Why is our training different?

The development of modern facial treatments has gained enormous momentum in recent years, especially in Poland and other Eastern European countries, where the professional standard of cosmetic treatments is exceptionally high.

During our training, we share a treatment approach and practical protocols built on international experience, with the help of which you can deliver genuine, striking results for your clients.

We believe that the success of a treatment does not depend on the device, but on the right knowledge, diagnostics and active-ingredient selection.

## Using your "hydra" machine, yet clients still aren't satisfied with the results?

Many people think that the success of a Hydro-Facial or AquaPeeling treatment is determined solely by the quality of the device, the power of the vacuum or the suction energy. The reality, however, is that the striking results stem primarily from the professional's knowledge.

The right pressure and suction force are of course important, but on their own they are not enough. The real difference comes down to the following:

- The correct cleansing technique
- The proper guidance of the handpiece
- Recognizing the skin type
- Selecting the right active ingredients
- The sequence of the treatment steps
- The treatment protocol applied
- The at-home care recommendations

A professional Hydro-Facial treatment does not consist of "running the handpiece across the skin." Behind successful treatments lie deliberate protocols, diagnostics and practical experience. In our training, we show you the techniques and professional tricks with which you can achieve truly striking results, and which keep your clients coming back.
