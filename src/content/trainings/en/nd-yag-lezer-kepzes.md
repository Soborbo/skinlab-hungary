Would you like to apply Q-switched ND:YAG / carbon laser technology confidently and safely? During our practice-oriented training, we provide comprehensive theoretical and practical knowledge so that you can use the device effectively for various aesthetic treatments.

## Structure of the training

### 1. Legal background

- Current regulations governing laser treatments
- Scope-of-competence limits and areas of responsibility
- Documentation obligations
- The fundamentals of safe working practice

### 2. Theoretical instruction

- The operating principle of the Q-switched ND:YAG laser
- Wavelengths and their fields of application
- The interaction of skin and pigments with laser light
- Indications and contraindications
- The correct selection of treatment parameters
- Possible side effects and their prevention

### 3. Practical instruction

During the practical part, we demonstrate and perform together the most popular treatments:

- Carbon Peeling (Hollywood Peel)
- Permanent makeup removal
- Lightening pigment spots
- Skin renewal treatments

We place special emphasis on correct technique and safe parameter settings.

### 4. Practice on a model

The model's presence is only required during the final hour of the training. **The participant is responsible for providing the model.**

Recommended treatments: Carbon Peeling, permanent makeup removal.

## What the training includes

- Detailed printed or digital course material
- Aftercare protocols
- Active-ingredient and product recommendations
- Treatment advice for maximum effectiveness
- Coffee and refreshments during the training

## Added benefit

The aftercare products, active ingredients and accessories used and recommended during the training are available from us in one place, so you can immediately start performing treatments professionally.

## Why is it worth learning the Carbon Peeling treatment?

Carbon Peeling is one of today's most popular facial treatments. With the right technique and parameters, striking results can be achieved:

- A more even complexion
- Cleaner pores
- A fresher, more radiant appearance
- More refreshed facial skin

During the training, we demonstrate the practical techniques with which the treatment can deliver truly striking results.
