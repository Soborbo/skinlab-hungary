Diode laser hair removal is one of the most sought-after aesthetic treatments today. The aim of our training is for participants not only to master operating the device, but to gain the kind of practical knowledge with which they can work safely, effectively and confidently in their everyday practice.

## Why is our training different?

In the laser aesthetic treatment market, it's clearly visible that certain countries are several years ahead in terms of treatment protocols, techniques and effectiveness. A particularly high-level laser treatment culture has developed in Poland, where professionals achieve outstanding results on a daily basis. Our training materials and treatment approach are largely built on international experience that Polish and Eastern European professionals have perfected over many years.

Our goal is for you not just to perform treatments, but to truly achieve results.

## Structure of the training

### 1. Legal background

- Regulations governing laser treatments
- Scope-of-competence limits
- Documentation obligations
- Occupational health and safety regulations
- Consent forms and client treatment

### 2. Theoretical instruction

#### How the diode laser works

- The principle of selective photothermolysis
- The role of the hair shaft and melanin
- The path of laser energy to the hair follicle
- Why can hair only be treated effectively in certain growth phases?

#### Skin and hair types

- Fitzpatrick skin types
- Hair thickness and pigmentation
- Developing an individual treatment strategy

#### Treatment parameters

- Energy (J/cm²)
- Pulse duration
- Frequency
- The role of cooling technologies
- Parameter selection for different skin and hair types

#### Indications and contraindications

- When can a treatment be performed safely?
- When must a treatment be postponed or declined?
- Medications and photosensitivity

### 3. Practical instruction

During the practical training, we demonstrate:

- The course of the consultation
- Condition assessment
- Preparing a treatment plan
- Correct handpiece movement
- Parameter-setting practice
- Performing full-body treatments
- The particularities of facial and intimate areas
- Common mistakes and how to avoid them

### 4. Practice on a model

The model's presence is only required during the final hour of the training. **The participant is responsible for providing the model.**

## What the training includes

## Aftercare protocols and product recommendations

Successful laser hair removal does not end when the treatment is complete. During the training, we cover in detail:

- Immediate aftercare tasks
- The importance of sun protection
- Recommended active ingredients
- Irritation-reducing products
- At-home skincare protocols

The products and accessories recommended during the training are available from us in one place.

## Why is it worth working with a diode laser?

Modern diode laser technology, when applied correctly:

- Provides long-term hair reduction
- Offers a more comfortable treatment experience for clients
- Allows for the fast treatment of larger areas
- Is an exceptionally sought-after service all year round

During the training, we show you the practical techniques and settings with which you can work safely and effectively. Learn the way successful professionals on the international market work, and build a stable, returning client base with professional laser treatments.
