If you want to work with a cosmetic device - whether a diode laser, ND:YAG laser or another professional device - you'll need a **FAR machine operator certificate**. But what exactly is it?

## What is the FAR certificate?

The FAR (Occupational Health and Safety) certificate is an **official document** that confirms you:

- Have mastered the safe operation of the device
- Know the treatment protocols
- Are aware of the contraindications
- Comply with occupational safety regulations

## Why is it mandatory?

### Legal background

In Hungary, the appropriate qualification is **required by law** to use professional cosmetic devices. Working without a machine operator certificate:

- Can result in a fine
- Can cause insurance problems
- Can endanger clients

### Business considerations

The FAR certificate:

- **Builds trust** with clients
- **Conveys professionalism**
- **Sets you apart** from untrained competitors
- May be an **insurance requirement**
