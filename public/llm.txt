# Skinlab Hungary

> Professional cosmetic and laser equipment distributor for beauty salons and clinics in Central Europe.

Skinlab Hungary (Skinlab Beauty Equipment Kft.) is a B2B/B2C distributor of professional cosmetic equipment based in Érd, Hungary. The company specializes in high-performance laser devices, hydrodermabrasion systems, cold plasma equipment, permanent makeup machines, and salon furniture. All equipment comes with a 2-year warranty, free FAR-certified operator training, and in-house repair service.
